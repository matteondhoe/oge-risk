The Aha Moment  ·  Solution Approach

Two Systems.
Same Users.

The physical and digital analyses were run completely independently. The critical and high-tier lists below were computed with no awareness of each other, yet they surface the same risk profile.

Key Finding
User 6976 holds 45 unassociated entitlements, more than any other identity in the dataset. These entitlements exist completely outside any assigned role or access profile. This same user scores in the top tier for after-hours badge activity. Two independent systems. One user. Zero prior visibility.
